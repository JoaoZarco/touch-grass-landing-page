Features

Everything you need to get outside more

Outdoor Time Tracking

Automatically tracks how much time you spend outside each day using your phone's sensors — no manual logging needed.

Smart Nudges

Receive meaningful, well-timed notifications that encourage you to step out and touch grass when the moment is right.

Daily & Weekly Insights

See your outdoor patterns over time. Understand your habits and celebrate your progress with clear, calming visuals.

Personal Goals

Set a daily outdoor time target that works for you, whether that's 30 minutes or 2 hours. We'll help you stay on track.

Minimal & Calm Design

No overwhelming dashboards. Just a clean, peaceful interface that respects your attention and keeps things simple.

Privacy First

Your location data stays on your device. We don't track where you go — only that you went outside.

How It Works

Three steps to a healthier routine

1

Download & set your goal

Install the app and choose how much outdoor time you'd like each day. Even 20 minutes makes a difference.

2

Go about your day

Touch Grass quietly tracks your outdoor time in the background. When you've been indoors too long, you'll get a friendly nudge.

3

Watch your habits improve

Review your daily and weekly trends. Over time, you'll naturally spend more time outside and feel the difference.
